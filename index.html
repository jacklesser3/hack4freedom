BUILD THE FUTURE

Join developers, activists, and innovators for an intensive weekend of building cutting-edge solutions that advance freedom, privacy, and decentralized technology.

WHAT TO EXPECT

AI Hack for Freedom brings together the brightest minds in the Bitcoin and Freedom Tech community to collaborate, innovate, and build the future of decentralized technology.

Whether you're a developer or activist, this hackathon is the perfect opportunity to work on meaningful projects, network with like-minded individuals, and contribute to the Freedom Tech ecosystem.

HOW IT WORKS

Activist-Led Innovation

Every team is led by an activist "captain" with a real-world problem to solve. Developers apply to work on activist-proposed solutions, creating meaningful impact from day one.

Team Structure

We're targeting a certain number of teams with one activist and one or two developers per team.

Prize Distribution

The 1 Bitcoin prize pool is distributed across multiple tracks. The grand prize winner receives 0.5 Bitcoin.

WEEKEND SCHEDULE

Join us for a Friday welcome reception,
followed by intensive building sessions on Saturday and Sunday.
Mentors will be available throughout the weekend to guide teams.
